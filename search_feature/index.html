Fruits Items

Apple

Crisp and sweet or tart, apples come in many varieties. They're rich in fiber and vitamin C. Commonly eaten raw, baked, or juiced.

Banana

Soft, sweet, and easy to peel, bananas are energy-rich. They’re high in potassium and good for digestion. Great as a snack or in smoothies and desserts.

Orange

Juicy and tangy, oranges are packed with vitamin C. They boost immunity and hydrate the body. Often eaten fresh or juiced.

Grapes

Small, juicy fruits that grow in bunches. They’re full of antioxidants and natural sugars. Enjoyed fresh, dried (raisins), or in wine.

Pineapple

Tropical fruit with a spiky skin and sweet, tangy flesh. Rich in vitamin C and digestive enzymes. Great in juices, salads, and grilled dishes.

Watermelon

Large and refreshing with high water content. Perfect for summer hydration. Sweet, juicy flesh with black seeds (or seedless varieties).

Mango

Known as the "king of fruits," mangoes are juicy and fragrant.
